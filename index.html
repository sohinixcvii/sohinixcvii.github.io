About Me

I am a PhD student at the Jodrell Bank Centre for Astrophysics – The University of Manchester. My current research interests lie in Cosmology and the very early universe, particularly in the areas of Inflation, Galaxy Evolution, Cosmic Dawn, and the Epoch of Reionization. If you would like to chat with me, send me an email!
